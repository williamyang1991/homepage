Hi, I'm Shuai Yang (杨 帅)

This is my personal website. I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Institute of Computer Science & Technology of Peking University (ICST). I'm a member of the Spatial and Temporal Restoration, Understanding and Compression Team (STRUCT) at ICST.

Experience

  • Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science (09/2015 - present)
    Institute of Computer Science and Technology, Peking University
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science (09/2010 - 07/2015)
    School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University

Research

My interest mainly lies in low-level image processing, including:

  • Image Comletion
  • Depth Enhancement
  • Image Stylization

My research projects are available at my project page.
